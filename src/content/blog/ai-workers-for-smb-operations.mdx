AI Workers are digital employees designed to own repetitive business workflows: triaging inboxes, qualifying leads, preparing reports, summarizing documents, and routing tasks to the right person.

For small and midsize businesses, the best first workflow is usually high-volume, rules-based, and measurable. Examples include customer response, proposal drafting, accounts receivable follow-up, CRM hygiene, and internal knowledge search.

The goal is not to replace strategic staff. The goal is to remove repetitive execution so your team can focus on customers, decisions, and growth.
